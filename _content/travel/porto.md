[Porto Airport
(OPO)](https://www.aeroportoporto.pt/en/opo/access-parking/airport-directions/location)
is connected to several big cities like: New York, Toronto, Montreal,
Rio, Sao Paulo, Londo, Frankfurt, Helsinki, Paris.

To get to the venue from Porto Airport:

-   **Rent a car** - 3 hours\
    Porto airport is about 300km away from A Coruña.

-   **Bus** - 4.5 - 6 hours\
    Fare: ~50€.\
    Payment method: Buy tickets from [FlixBus](https://www.flixbus.com),
    [Alsa](https://www.alsa.com/coach/porto-a-coruna) or compare options using
    [Busbud](https://www.busbud.com).\
    When searching for tickets, look for departures from Porto Airport if you
    want the simplest option. Some routes leave directly from the airport, while
    others depart from Porto city and would require you to take
    [the metro](https://en.metrodoporto.pt) before getting on the bus.

-   **Train + Train** - 2.5 hours + 1.5 hours\
    Cost: 25-65€\
    Start by traveling from the airport to Porto’s main train station
    (Campanhã). You can take [the metro](https://en.metrodoporto.pt) from
    Porto Airport.\
    From there, book a train from Porto to Vigo on
    [the Celta international train](https://www.cp.pt).\
    Check arrival time in Vigo to ensure there will be a connection to A Coruña
    leaving Vigo when you arrive. Once confirmed that there will be a second
    train that works with your first, then you may book a second train from
    Vigo to A Coruña using [Renfe](https://www.renfe.com).\
    Allow about 45–60 minutes to get from the airport to Campanhã, and at least
    30–60 minutes in Vigo to make your connection.

-   **Bus + Train** - 1.5-2 hours + 1.5 hours\
    Cost: 20-60€\
    Start by booking a bus from Porto (ideally Porto Airport) to Vigo using
    [FlixBus](https://www.flixbus.com), [Alsa](https://www.alsa.com) or
    [Autna](https://autna.com/en/). Then book a train from Vigo to A Coruña
    using [Renfe](https://www.renfe.com).\
    Choose a bus time that allows enough buffer after your flight, and leave at
    least 30–60 minutes between your arrival in Vigo and your train departure.\
    When booking your train, look for departures from Vigo Urzaiz, which is the
    most convenient station, closest to bus arrivals and where the quickest
    trains to A Coruña depart from.
