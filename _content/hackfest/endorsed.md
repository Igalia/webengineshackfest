The Web Engines Hackfest is a conferenced endorsed by the [World Wide Web Consortium (W3C)](https://www.w3.org/events/conferences/2026/2026-web-engines-hackfest/) and part of [W3C's official calendar](https://www.w3.org/events/). Some W3C working groups meet at the event.
