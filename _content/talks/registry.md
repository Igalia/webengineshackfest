WinterCG (now TC55 in Ecma) needed a runtime keys registry, but nobody wanted to operate registry infrastructure. This talk explains how TC55 solved that problem by publishing the registry as an Ecma Technical Report instead — avoiding the operational burden while still delivering a usable, standards-backed resource.
